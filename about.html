ABOUT US

Expand Happiness,Beyond Boundaries

Smart Restaurant is a unique application developed by EIL for mitigating the pain areas of Restaurant Owners after thoroughly studying their processes and requirement. This application will totally eliminate the Manual working and Digitalize the entire process. Further this application also takes care of various remedial measures for Covid-19 particularly minimum human contacts. With this application the waiter allocates the table, take the order on the table itself digitally and the same will automatically get transmitted to kitchen and the manager so the chances of manual errors will get completely eliminated. The manager will be in a position to capture attendance, track inventory, do trend analysis, performance analysis with this application. The end user mobile app will be helpful for brand building including customer retention by way of discounts/royalty incentives.

Efficacious is synonym to Efficient and our vision is to create & provide Innovative/ Cost effective solution to the complex needs of Customers’ to make them feel Safe, Secure & Comfortable. EIL is a company is a company incorporated in 2012 with a clear vision to create & provide innovative IT solutions for selective target segments. In the initial years, the Company was engaged solely in IT outsourcing activities to establish its presence in the market and simultaneously developing its own product. Apart from Smart Restaurant app, EIL has also developed E-Smarts which has been developed as a complete end-to-end solution for Schools/ Education Institutes, which shall completely digitalize the operations with the much needed Child Safety/tracking also as the prime focus.

The Company is working with a clear vision, to be reckoned as one of the leading Global players in IT space providing latest Technological Solution at the most competitive pricing to eradicate issues being faced by the common man. Our aim is to continuously Identify, Develop and Market innovative products for the common man in our target segment to eventually emerge as one of the most trusted name in providing IT solutions. Further the company has been accredited with ISO 9001-2015 certification as a mark towards standard process flow and also got external rating done by CARE ratings, to showcase our financial stability. We have a team of both experiences and young/enthusiastic people who continuously work towards achieving company’s vision.
